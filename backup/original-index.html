Our priorities

Where technology meets human need

We focus on three areas where smart, well-designed technology can change everyday life for the better.

01

Mobility

Cleaner, smarter, more accessible ways for people and communities to move — connecting places without the cost to the planet.

02

Health

Technology that makes care more available, more preventive, and more human — closing gaps between people and the help they need.

03

Energy

Sustainable energy solutions that empower communities — efficient, renewable, and built to last for the generations that follow.

About us

We build with society, not just for it.

TIS Association — Technology and Innovation for Society — is a non-profit working at the intersection of innovation and the public good.

We believe the best technology is the kind people barely notice because it simply makes life better. That conviction guides everything from the projects we choose to the partners we work with.
